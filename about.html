Rebuilding the Employment Layer.

Trajectory exists to move hiring from documents to structured intelligence.

The Problem

Modern hiring is built on legacy artifacts. Resumes were created for paper. Hiring is now digital, global, and algorithmic.

The Vision

Structured applicant identity. Transparent evaluation. Intelligence that amplifies humans — not replaces them.

Founder

Trajectory was built from firsthand experience navigating broken hiring systems. The goal is simple: bring clarity, fairness, and signal back to employment.
